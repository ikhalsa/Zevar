About

The ultimate house of brilliant craftsmanship showcasing an extensive collection of outstanding Jewelery items studded with precious stones. We, at Zevar, are trusted name for designing and developing authentic Jewelery products. We are a leading manufacturer, exporter, importer, supplier, and trader of beautifully designed Jewelery items since 1970.

Quality Control

We let purity, authenticity and originality speak louder than the words, we would be putting forth in appreciation of our quality control process. At Zevar, each stage of manufacturing, processing, designing and finishing is closely monitored by a panel of leading craftsmen & designers.

Our well equipped in-house inspection cell is manned by a team of experienced quality control personnel, who check every product minutely before sending it to display or delivering it to the customers.

We pride in carrying the largest variety of 22 kt Gold, Diamond and Gemstone Jewelry in entire Northern America. We offer superior quality and largest selection in Gemstone jewelry, Diamond Jewelry, Gold and Antique Jewelry. Though, 22kt Gold Jewelry in our forte we also sell 18 kt Gold jewelry. Our Gemstone Jewelry is handcrafted to perfection and is also backed by fantastic workmanship and extended warranty.

Diamonds are very close to our hearts and we love to get that special place. So we sell the best quality (IF, VVS/VS Clarity) of dazzling diamonds with an unbeatable warranty. Zevar is a reliable one-stop-shop where you can buy GIA, GAL, EGL, IGI Certified Diamonds.

We also have an ethnic collection of Antique Jewelry for all occasions and tastes. This collection has been traditionally designed to adorn you and complement just a bit so that you look stunning and spectacular just like our jewelry.
